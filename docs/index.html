Reform
Congress

Reform Congress is a non-partisan group that promotes:

  • A common-sense Congress that accepts their Constitutional responsibilities
  • Laws that are clear in language
  • Fiscal responsibility, Transparent budgets
  • Removal of barriers to personal and economic growth
  • Term limits for representatives
  • Inclusion of third-party and Independent Congress members
